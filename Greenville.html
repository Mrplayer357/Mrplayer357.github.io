GREENVILLE, SOUTH CAROLINA

Greenville is a large city located in the North-Western region of South Carolina and has an estimated population of 519,178. Greenville was officially incorporated in the year of 1831. According to databusa.io ,Greenville's median household income in 2021 was around $66,000 - Considerably higher than the average median household income of South Carolina that sits around $58,000.
